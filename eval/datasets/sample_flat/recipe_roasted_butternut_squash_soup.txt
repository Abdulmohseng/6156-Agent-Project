RECIPE: Roasted Butternut Squash Soup
Servings: 6  |  Prep: 15 min  |  Cook: 45 min  |  Total: 1 hr

INGREDIENTS
  - 1 large butternut squash (~3 lbs), halved and seeded
  - 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  - 4 garlic cloves, minced
  - 2 cups vegetable broth
  - 1 cup coconut milk
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp ground cumin
  - 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  - Salt and black pepper to taste
  - Pumpkin seeds and cream for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Brush squash halves with olive oil, season with salt.
  2. Place cut-side down on baking sheet. Roast 35–40 min until fork-tender.
  3. Scoop out squash flesh. Set aside.
  4. In a large pot, sauté onion in olive oil over medium heat for 5 min.
  5. Add garlic, cumin, paprika, cayenne. Cook 1 min until fragrant.
  6. Add squash, broth. Simmer 10 min.
  7. Blend with immersion blender until completely smooth.
  8. Stir in coconut milk. Adjust seasoning.
  9. Serve topped with pumpkin seeds and a drizzle of cream.

NOTES: Freezes well for up to 3 months. Add a Granny Smith apple while roasting for sweetness.
